Our solution is a user-friendly reader, that uses one small drop of blood and gives precise results within 15 min for multi-parameter test panels.

Our platform combines different detection modules and will soon have the potential to perform the vast majority of blood tests available in the form of different types of test panels (spinit® - disposable tests).

Point-of-Care

Perform clinical assays right in the doctors office, using only one drop of blood.

Fast result times

Get your results during your consultation, no more waiting around for the central lab.
